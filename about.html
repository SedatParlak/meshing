Vision Statement

Our Vision is to sustain and develop a team of highly skilled professionals that achieve our customers’ construction needs with honesty and integrity. To provide an environment that promotes personal growth and self-pride. Ultimately, to differentiate ourselves as an independent construction partner of choice to those seeking quality, innovation, best value and a professional service.

Mission Statement

Meshing is a leader in providing value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process. Our intent is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through the exceptional performance of our company staff.

Who We Are & What We Do

Established in 1900, Meshing is one of the Turkey’s oldest independent construction companies. We are a respected name in the civilengineering and construction markets to both Public and Private sector customers. For almost 150-years we have successfully contributed to the built environment, delivering innovative, high-quality and sustainable projects. We are a major local employer providing knowledge and expertise from our 200-strong workforce, which operates from our network of regional ofﬁces. We provide our customers with a unique construction service, through the synergy between our Building and Civil Engineering departments.
